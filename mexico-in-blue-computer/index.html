Mexico In

Blue

Mexico in Blue is A Social Enterprise striving to solve the water crisis in Mexico. We distribute and install water filters so that the underprivileged Mexicans can collect rainwater, tapwater and riverwater, filter it, and have a constant supply for the whole family.
