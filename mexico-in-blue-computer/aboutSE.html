Hello!

We are Mexico In Blue. Our company is a Social Entreprise, meaning that our goal is to solve a problem, and not to make money. We sell our water, and then spend 60% of our money to buy water filters, adn give them to the people of Mexico City, where water is scarce. We are the 3 founders of Mexico In Blue: Vladislav Kooltnikov, Isabella Leisgold, and Yuliya Polishnuk. We are a small group of teenagers with a vision of how to improve the world we live in. We met on the Stanford campus during a Social Enterprise camp, and have been working on this project ever since. We are really glad to get a chance to improve the world, and save lives.
