Equal Opportunity

We believe that water is a fundamental human right. Every person must have unlimited access to fresh drinking water no matter their past, present, or future, no matter their social or economic status, no matter their religious or political beliefs. Unfortunately, in many countries around the globe, people have to fight for water. Different criminal organizations use water scarcity as an opportunity for their benefit. Our goal is to end this corruption once, and for all, and provide what the people deserve. We hold it to be self-evident that every single human is born with a right to such essentials like water, and we will not stop until we ensure that this dream comes true. Let's save lives together

Here are some facts about the water situation in Mexico right now.

  • The Population of Mexico City is 21 million. At least a fifth of them do not have reliable tap water service.
  • $100 billion Amount spent on an ambitious affordable-housing project since 2005. Many developers cut corners, leaving neighborhoods vulnerable to floods and grappling with failed water and sewer systems.
  • 40 percent Proportion of water that is lost due to leaks and theft as it runs through the city’s 8,000 miles of pipes
  • 40 percent Proportion of Mexico City’s water that is piped in from remote sources
